#### Senior Legal Advisor
Adnane Belahcen has experience advising sponsors and public sector entities in Africa, in the energy and infrastructure sectors. Before founding Lincoln Legal Services, he practiced for several years in Paris and London with renowned British and American law firms including Allen & Overy, Chadbourne & Parke, and Shearman & Sterling. He also held the position of Director of Legal Affairs at ONEE.

Adnane has worked on numerous projects in North Africa and Sub-Saharan Africa. Adnane has been assisting GPM Holding in all legal and contractual matters since 2017, including the drafting and negotiation of project contracts (PPA, EPC, O&M) and financing agreements.
