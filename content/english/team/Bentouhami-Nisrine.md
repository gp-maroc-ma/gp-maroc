#### Civil Engineer
Nisrine currently holds the position of Civil Engineer within the Construction Department of GPM Holding. She ensures the technical monitoring of solar park projects, participates in their design, particularly on aspects related to foundations and the stability of solar structures according to the conditions of different sites, and ensures the monitoring of studies carried out during the project development phase. She is currently preparing for the launch of construction sites for the GPM2 and GPM4 projects.

She graduated from the National School of Applied Sciences of Al Hoceima (ENSA-H), where she obtained the title of State Engineer in Civil Engineering.
