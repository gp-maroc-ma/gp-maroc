#### Deputy General Manager GPM Holding
Salima Bouhriz joined GPM Holding in 2019 as Director of Photovoltaic Panel Production Units. She is currently developing the Solaris Tangier factory with an annual capacity of 700MW. She oversees the monitoring of technological developments in panels and industrial integration in the manufacturing process. Salima previously worked at a consulting firm in Philadelphia, USA. She holds a degree in Civil and Environmental Engineering from Villanova University.
