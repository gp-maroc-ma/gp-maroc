#### Founder and CEO of Almaden Nigeria
CEO of Almaden Nigeria. He is in charge of developing the group's solar activity in Africa and inaugurated the first commercialization of solar panels manufactured in Morocco in Africa. He has over 20 years of experience in managing public and private companies.

He also has extensive experience in improving service delivery to corporate clients. Dapo holds a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering from Fairleigh Dickinson University.
