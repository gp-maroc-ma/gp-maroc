#### Financial and Administrative Assistant
Financial and Administrative Assistant within the financial department of GPM HOLDING, she ensures budget monitoring, processing and payment of invoices related to various project milestones, cash flow monitoring, preparation of accounting elements, and administrative coordination, including exchanges with the bank for operational needs. She also contributes to the HR management of the operating company, including the integration of new recruits and employee payroll.

She holds a degree in International Trade from the National School of Commerce and Management of Agadir (ENCG-A).
