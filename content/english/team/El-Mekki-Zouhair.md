#### Technical Director
Zouhair currently holds the position of Technical Director at GPM Holding, where he is responsible for overseeing the technical activities of solar photovoltaic projects within the company. He notably led the study, implementation, and commissioning of the GreenPower Morocco 1 solar photovoltaic park, with a capacity of 30 MWac, in Tangier, connected to the Medium Voltage network. He graduated from the National School of Electricity and Mechanics as a State Engineer.

Zouhair worked on other similar projects before joining GPM Holding, notably on the development and implementation of Noor Tafilalt and on the development of Noor Atlas at ONE.
