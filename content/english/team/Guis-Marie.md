#### Senior Legal Advisor
Marie began her career in the United States before joining the Project Development and Finance team at Shearman & Sterling in Paris. She has advised numerous investors, investment banks, and public institutions on energy and infrastructure projects. Marie has worked on numerous projects in France and Africa. She notably advised Africa50 on its minority equity investment in the project company NHPC as part of the Nachtigal project. Marie also intervened to assist GPM Holding in the context of the first project developed as an IPP in Morocco, particularly on aspects related to financing.

She has an initial academic background in public law. Marie has been assisting GPM Holding in all legal and contractual matters since 2017.
