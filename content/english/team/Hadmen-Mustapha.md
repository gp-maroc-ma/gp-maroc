#### Construction Manager
Construction Manager at GPM HOLDING, he brings 16 years of expertise as a civil and mechanical engineer on major large-scale projects such as the realization of the first high-speed rail line (LGV). His role encompasses the complete supervision of projects, from the design phase to execution, where he masters team coordination to ensure strict compliance with deadlines and the most demanding quality standards. He supervised the mechanical and civil works of the GreenPower Morocco 1 photovoltaic power plant, with a capacity of 30 MWac, connected to the Medium Voltage network.

He holds a higher education degree in civil engineering and expertise from FST TANGIER, and also obtained a master's degree in geotechnical expertise from Mohammed Premier University in Oujda.
