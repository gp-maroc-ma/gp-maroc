#### Works Engineer
Yassine currently holds the position of Works Engineer within the Construction Department of GPM Holding. He is in charge of the technical monitoring of solar park projects. He also supervises civil engineering works as well as electrical connection works, from solar parks to substations. He is currently preparing for the launch of construction sites for the GPM2 and GPM4 projects.

Holder of a Master's degree in Civil Engineering, he graduated from the Faculty of Sciences and Techniques of Tangier.
