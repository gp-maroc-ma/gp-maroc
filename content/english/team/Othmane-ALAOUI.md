#### Project Finance Advisor
With over 20 years of experience in Project Finance, Othmane has been assisting GPM Holding since late 2023 in the structuring and financing of projects developed by the group. After spending 10 years in the EMEA Project Finance teams of BNP Paribas in Paris, Othmane returned to Morocco in 2015, where he has since been involved in the development, structuring, and financing of several infrastructure and energy projects for various clients and stakeholders.

Othmane is a graduate of HEC Paris and holds a DEA in Financial Macroeconomics from the Sorbonne, where he also took courses for the DEA in African Studies, Political Science option.
