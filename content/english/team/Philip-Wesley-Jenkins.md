#### Founder, Principal Shareholder and CEO of GPM Holding
Mr. Philip Wesley Jenkins is an American investor who has made numerous investments in both the renewable energy sector, through the GPM1 project in Tangier, and in the digital marketing sector in Morocco, particularly in the north of the country, creating 400 direct jobs in Al Hoceima and 1500 in Tangier. Mr. Jenkins is an international entrepreneur very active in the United States, India, and Morocco.

In addition to investments made in the development of photovoltaic solar parks, Mr. Jenkins invested 120 million dirhams in 2024 in the industrial photovoltaic solar panel assembly unit, with an annual capacity of 700 MWp, located in Tangier. Through this, he intends to position himself as a true partner of public authorities in their desire to develop the economic and industrial fabric of this region.

Passionate about the environment and aware of the importance of the role of clean energy in the Kingdom's development, Philip has introduced innovative technology while participating in the diversification of the energy matrix and the social development of the country. Philip is a graduate of the international schools of management and business administration at Roosevelt University, Chicago.
