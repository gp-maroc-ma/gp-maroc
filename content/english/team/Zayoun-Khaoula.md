#### Deputy General Manager GPM Holding
In charge of the development of the group's photovoltaic projects since 2017. She leads the various aspects of the development of GPM Holding's solar activity, ensuring the launch and monitoring of technical and economic studies, the management of administrative procedures necessary for the start of projects, the negotiation of project contracts and financing agreements. Holding an engineering degree from the Institut National Polytechnique de Grenoble (INPG) in Energy Systems Engineering and Markets in France, Khaoula began her career in Belgium with the development of electricity production projects from biomass.

She has also worked on various projects related to energy efficiency in buildings and the reduction of energy consumption for several industrial companies.
